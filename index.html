Eyedit Group

Eyedit Group is an international strategic holding built around three defined projects: Eyedit Image Quick Service, focused on audiovisual production, radio, television, streaming, model agency and talent scouting; CCSCANSSAM, focused on natural medicine, alternative therapies and wellness; and EyeVision, focused on air, naval and land transport, cyber security and private security.

Holding Vision

Three projects, one strategic direction.

Eyedit Group is conceived as a premium international holding where each project has a concrete role, a distinct market field and its own expansion path, while contributing to one unified corporate ecosystem.

Eyedit Image Quick Service

Eyedit Image Quick Service is the audiovisual and communication division of the holding. It is focused on audiovisual production, radio, television, streaming, model agency activities and talent scouting, creating a platform for image, visibility and media projection.

CCSCANSSAM

CCSCANSSAM is the wellness and natural health division. It is oriented toward natural medicine, alternative therapies and wellness experiences, building a space for care, balance and human-centered services.

EyeVision

EyeVision is the operations and protection division. It includes air, naval and land transport, cyber security and private security, designed for strategic support, control and operational reliability.
